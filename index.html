About BMED

Simplifying Healthcare
Access in Africa

Bridge Medical Network (BMED) is a digital platform connecting patients with verified, trusted hospitals across Nigeria — and soon, across Africa.

Our Mission

Simplify healthcare access by linking patients with verified hospitals quickly and efficiently across Nigeria and Africa.

Our Vision
